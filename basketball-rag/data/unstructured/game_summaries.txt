Game 1 vs UC Irvine (Nov 6, 2023):
The UCLA Women's Basketball team started their season strong with an 89-65 victory over UC Irvine. The Bruins showcased their offensive firepower with four players scoring in double digits. The team's defense was particularly impressive, recording 12 steals and forcing 20 turnovers.

Game 2 vs Pepperdine (Nov 9, 2023):
In a high-scoring affair, UCLA defeated Pepperdine 92-78. The Bruins dominated the paint, outrebounding their opponents 48-32. The team's three-point shooting was exceptional, hitting 12 of 31 attempts from beyond the arc.

Game 3 vs USC (Nov 12, 2023):
The Bruins faced their crosstown rivals USC in a tightly contested match. Despite a strong defensive effort, UCLA fell short 78-72. The team struggled with turnovers, committing 18 compared to USC's 12. However, the Bruins showed resilience, cutting a 15-point deficit to just 4 in the final minutes.

Game 4 vs Stanford (Nov 15, 2023):
UCLA bounced back with an impressive 85-70 victory over Stanford. The Bruins' balanced scoring attack was led by their senior guard who contributed 22 points and 6 assists. The team's defense held Stanford to just 38% shooting from the field.

Game 5 vs California (Nov 18, 2023):
The Bruins continued their winning ways with a 91-75 victory over California. UCLA's fast-paced offense was too much for the Golden Bears to handle, as the team recorded 23 assists on 35 made field goals. The Bruins' bench provided valuable contributions, scoring 28 points.
